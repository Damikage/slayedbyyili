Booking – Terms & Conditions


1. Appointments All appointments must be booked at least 48 hours in advance.
A £5 non-refundable deposit is required to secure your slot.
Late arrivals beyond 15 minutes may result in cancellation.

2. Cancellations & Rescheduling Please give at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling.
Failure to do so will result in the deposit being forfeited.

3. Payments Full payment is due immediately after the service.
Cash, bank transfer, and other agreed methods are accepted.

4. Client Responsibility Please arrive with your hair clean and detangled, unless you've booked a wash and prep service.
Additional fees may apply if prep is needed but not booked.

5. No-Shows Clients who miss an appointment without prior notice will be required to pay in full before rebooking in the future.

6. Refund Policy Due to the nature of the service, refunds are not provided.
If you have concerns, please raise them before leaving the appointment.

7. Photo & Video Consent Photos and videos may be taken during or after your appointment for social media and promotional purposes.
By booking, you agree to this unless you state otherwise before your appointment.

8. Gift Boxes & Offers Gift items are subject to availability and are complimentary.
Offers and discounts cannot be combined unless stated.

By booking with Slayed by Yili, you agree to these terms and conditions.
