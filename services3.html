Style Guide

Here is a detailed style guide that outlines the different hairstyles we create, showcasing the variety and expertise we offer to help you choose the perfect look

BOX BRAIDS

A classic protective style where your natural hair is braided into extensions. Box braids are lightweight, versatile, and perfect for long-lasting, low-maintenance beauty.

TWISTS

A beautiful and elegant protective style where hair is twisted into sleek or chunky sections. Twists are great for adding volume, length, and texture.

BUTTERFLY LOCS

A trendy and boho-inspired style, butterfly locs create a messy, textured, and natural look that’s lightweight and stylish. Perfect for a carefree, chic vibes
